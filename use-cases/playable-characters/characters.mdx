Equip your NFT players with onchain identity and inventory, powered by NFT Accounts.

### Create or choose the NFT Collection 

1. Go to the ‘Accounts’ tab in the Dashboard.
2. Click ‘+ New Account Group’.
3. Fill out the Account Group details. Click ‘Next’.

![Accounts.png](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/fe791874-a65e-4b97-be8b-9203bb4c0596.png)

4. In the ‘Connected NFT’ section, choose an existing NFT collection or create a new one. Click ‘Done’ to create the NFT collection and the Account Group.

<Info>Currently only support ERC-721 NFTs</Info>
![Accounts.png](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/bad9cd44-dfce-4c48-9f77-a3bdb623ff89.png)

Use GroupOS to enable gasless user experience and expressive user identity for your applications, from onboarding and beyond. 

Smart Accounts are ERC-4337 compatible.


### Core features
<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="Log in with Station">
        Enable users to connect with your application using Station Accounts or create a new Smart Accounts with a few lines of code.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Gas Policies">
        Onboard your users gaslessly and sponsor gas for selected users.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Session Keys">
        Enable signatureless transactions and/or automate transactions for seamless user experience using Session Keys.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Batch Transactions">
        Execute transactions in batches to skip the need to sign and spend gas multiple times. 
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Directory">
        Track Accounts that have interacted with your applications, rewards they earn, and assets they hold.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Analytics">
        Measure your user growth and monitor the gas spend to inform your growth strategy.
    </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Extended features
Powered by <a href="/smart-accounts/overview">Extensions</a>
<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="Points">
        Reward users with onchain points and optionally unlock new market dynamics with tradable Points (ERC-20).
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Posts">
        Enable users to bring their actions onchain by minting them as Posts (ERC-1155).
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Badges">
        Segment your users based on usage and participation with Badges (ERC-1155).
    </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

An Attestation is a digital record that is cryptographically signed by an individual, company, or group to verify the identity, credentials, or other information about an individual, organization, or entity. Attest to members’ actions to verify their authenticity. 

Attestations are powered by **[Ethereum Attestation Service** (EAS)](https://attest.sh/).

**Why attestations?**

- A means to establish trust and credibility online
- Developers can easily integrate attestations into their projects and benefit from the interoperability and composability of the attestation layer

**Use cases**

- Reward ownership verification
- KYC/AML compliance
- Member identity verification
- Digital credentials
- Skills verification

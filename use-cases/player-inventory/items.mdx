title: "Items"
---

Items are ERC-1155-compatible NFTs for in-game items like weapons, equipments, and collectibles.

### Create an Items Collection

1. Go to the ‘Tokens’ tab in the Dashboard.
2. Click ‘+ New Token’. Select ‘ERC-1155’.
3. Fill out the details and configure transferability. Click ‘Done’.

![ERC-1155.png](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/8a4cc1d6-8ef6-429b-85ae-a8f7c479c805.png)


### Distribute Items

After [setting up your API Keys](/introduction/setting-up) and creating the Items Collection, you can start distributing Badges to your members.

Entry forms enable you to collect relevant member information and associate with their onchain accounts.

### Add entry form

1. Click **+ New collection** and go through the flow
2. On the Mint Portal step, enable **Collection member information** and select the information you want to collect from members
    
    ![Screen Shot 2023-08-13 at 4.21.30 PM.png](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/754f2aed-9c6f-468e-88ca-bf7b24a778c7/Screen_Shot_2023-08-13_at_4.21.30_PM.png)
    

### Edit entry form

1. Go to **Members** tab on **Dashboard** and select a collection
2. Click the [gear] icon and go to **Settings**
3. In the Mint Portal section, make changes to the information you want to collect from members

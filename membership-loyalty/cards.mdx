title: "Cards"
---

Cards are unique dynamic NFTs that evolve with your member’s identity, powered by [NFT Accounts](/nft-accounts/overview). 

### Create and configure Cards

1. Go to the ‘Accounts’ tab in the Dashboard.
2. Click ‘+ New Account Group’.
3. Fill out the Account Group details. Click ‘Next’.
<Info>If you look to deploy a test collection, select Goerli.</Info>

![Accounts.png](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/fe791874-a65e-4b97-be8b-9203bb4c0596.png)

4. Optionally, sponsor gas for your members by setting the maxinmum spend per account in the ‘Gas Policies’ section.

![Accounts.png](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/66758136-44ae-4f5c-9565-8c817f4a56b1.png)

5. In the ‘Connected NFT’ section, customize the visuals and details of the NFTs. Click ‘Done’ to create the NFT collection and the Account Group.

![Accounts.png](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/bad9cd44-dfce-4c48-9f77-a3bdb623ff89.png)

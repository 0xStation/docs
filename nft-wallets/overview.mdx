title: "Overview"
---

Enable your users to own digital assets and transact onchain with NFTs by equip smart wallets to NFTs.

NFT Wallet are ERC-6551 and ERC-4337 compatible.

## Core

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="APIs">
    Automatically mint, update NFT metadata, and batch execute transactions
    based on offchain events with NFT Wallet APIs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Brandable Claim Page">
    Design and customize the Claim Page ("Claim your onchain profile") to match
    with your brand and collect members' information. Claim Portal comes with
    the ability to configure access list and offchain information you look to
    collect from your members.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Gas Policies">Sponsor gas for selected members.</Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Directory">
    Track wallets that are bounded to your NFT collections, rewards they earn,
    and assets they hold.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Analytics">
    Measure your member growth and monitor the gas spend to inform your growth
    strategy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Extensions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Points">
    Reward members with onchain points and optionally unlock new market dynamics.
    with tradable Points (ERC-20).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Badges">
    Segment and reward members based on usage and participation with Badges.
    (ERC-1155).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Portal">
    Enable users to connect with your applications using NFT Accounts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Use cases

- Member Passport
- Governance
- Gas Pass
- Playable Characters (e.g. Azuki's inventory)

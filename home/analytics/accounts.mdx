The dashboard provides an overview of activities in your smart contract wallet. It shows Unique Accounts, Active Accounts, and Account Retention. These categories display the number of individual wallets, engaged wallets, and ongoing utilization of the protocol or dApp. 

The dashboard simplifies complex on-chain data into easy-to-understand product analytics. It provides insights about ROI and growth. The dashboard is a useful resource for community and product leaders to make informed decisions and strategic plans.

title: "Points"
---

Points are ERC-20 tokens used to reward your member in numeric values over time.

### Create a Points Collection

1. Go to the ‘Tokens’ tab in the Dashboard.
2. Click ‘+ New Token’. Select ‘ERC-20’.
3. Fill out the details and configure transferability. Click ‘Done’.

![ERC-20.png](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/c42ffaa9-1d72-405d-9d5f-9ab0fee0fd7c.png)

### Distribute Points

After [setting up your API Keys](/introduction/quickstart) and creating the Points Collection, you can start distributing Points to your members.

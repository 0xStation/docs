title: "XP"
---

Distribute non-transferrable ERC-20 tokens to account for your players' in-game reputation.

### Create a XP Collection

1. In the Dashboard, go to the **Tokens** tab.
2. Click **'+ New Tokens'** and select **'ERC-20'** option
3. Fill out the token details and click **'Next'**.
    - Toggle **non-transferable** on to bound the XP to the player's Account.
4. Review the details and click **'Done'**.
5. Find and copy the collection contract address.

### Distribute XP

After [setting up your API Keys](/introduction/quickstart) and creating the XP Collection, you can start distributing XP to your members.

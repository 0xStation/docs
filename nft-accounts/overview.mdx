Equip each NFT with a smart contract wallet to boost its utility and reward members. 

NFT Accounts are ERC-6661 and ERC-4337 compatible.

### Core
<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="Brandable Claim Portal">
        Design and customize to the Claim Portal ("Claim your onchain profile") to match with your brand and collect members' information. Claim Portal comes with the ability to configure access list and offchain information you look to collect from your members.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="APIs">
        Automatically mint, update NFT metadata, and batch execute transactions based on offchain events with NFT Account APIs.  
   </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Gas Policies">
        Sponsor gas for selected members.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Directory">
        Track Accounts that are bounded to your NFT collections, rewards they earn, and assets they hold.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Analytics">
        Measure your member growth and monitor the gas spend to inform your growth strategy.
    </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Extensions
<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="Points">
        Reward members with onchain points and optionally unlock new market dynamics with tradable Points (ERC-20).
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Badges">
        Segment and reward members based on usage and participation with Badges (ERC-1155).
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Attestations">
        Cryptographically sign data to approve and verify member actions. 
    </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>


### Use cases
- Member Passport
- Governance
- Gas Pass
- Player Inventory (e.g. Azuki's inventory)

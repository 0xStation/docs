Equip Smart Accounts to your NFTs in a few minutes.

## Select an NFT collection

Select an NFT collection to equip Smart Accounts.

If you don't have an NFT collection, learn how to create a new NFT collection [here](/tokens/erc-721/new-collection).

1. In your Dashboard, go to **Tokens** tab.
2. Click **'+ New Tokens'**.
3. Select **'ERC-721'**.
![erc721](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/4b9f6d2c-7958-4cdf-b01e-df681af07a76.png)
4. Fill in your token information and toggle **'Enable TBA'** on.
![toggle](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/c83a6b1a-ea41-4e2e-b87f-a131899a2c9f.png)

### Direct transfer
You can directly transfer tokens into the Accounts from your wallets.
    - In your Dashboard, go to **Accounts** tab.
    - Find the Account and copy the Account address.
    ![Directory](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/4ea34fb1-0724-4b19-a3e3-3f75441b50ea.png)
    - Use the Account address as a recipient address in the wallet app of your choice.

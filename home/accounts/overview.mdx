GroupOS's Accounts are ERC-6551 and ERC-4337 compliant smart contract wallets (also known as 'NPCs' and 'Tokenbound Accounts'), offering full programmability and extensibility. 

We provide out-of-the-box toolkit groups need to gaslessly onboard and activate accounts to games, applications, and/or protocols. The toolkit includes:

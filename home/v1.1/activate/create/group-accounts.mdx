Group Accounts enables you to create multi-player accounts for your network. Powered by multi-signature, group accounts allow signers to propose, vote, and execute decisions together under a shared identity. 

**Use cases**

- Multiplayer decentralized social accounts
- Multiplayer game characters
- Project team accounts for permit platforms
- Vaults 

Group Accounts are currently in private beta. [Get on the list](https://6vdcjqzyfj3.typeform.com/to/x3xer1Rs) or contact staff@station.express.

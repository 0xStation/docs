---
title: "Publish Directory"
---

Publish the Directory to allow anyone to view holders and share the Directory.

### Publish

1. In your Dashboard, go to the **Tokens** tab.
2. Select a token.
3. Click the **ellipsis icon** on the top right and click **'Publish'**.
3. Click **'Publish'** to confirm.
4. Click **'Copy link'** to share.


### Unpublish

1. In your Dashboard, go to the Tokens tab.
2. Select a token.
3. Click the **ellipsis icon** on the top right and click **'Uppublish'**.

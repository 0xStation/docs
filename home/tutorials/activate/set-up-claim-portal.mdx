Customize the Claim Portal in a way that aligns with their brand vision.

After you've [deployed an Account collection](https://docs.groupos.xyz/home/tutorials/activate/deploy-accounts-collection), you can edit the Claim Portal anytime.

1. Go to Accounts tab in your Dashboard

2. Select a collection you want to update the Claim Portal

3. Find and click on settings icon 

4. Under General tab, navigate to Claim Portal section and make the changes

5. Click **Save**

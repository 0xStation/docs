GroupOS is live on Ethereum and Polygon. Base, Linea, and Optimism are scheduled to be added in October 2023.

To evaluate your network needs, [schedule a short call](https://calendly.com/symmtry/chat) with our team.

Tokens are uniquely identified by the combination of their smart contract address and the network it resides,
often labeled with `contractAddress` and `chainId` repspectively. If you are uncertain about the `chainId` of
your network, refer to [this list](https://chainlist.org/).

### Sandbox

You can do test-runs for free using by deploying the collection on Goerli. Get Goerli ETH from the available faucets.
- [QuickNode faucet](https://faucet.quicknode.com/ethereum/goerli)
- [Coinbase faucet](https://coinbase.com/faucets/ethereum-goerli-faucet)
- [Alchemy faucet](https://goerlifaucet.com/)
- [Paradigm faucet](https://faucet.paradigm.xyz/)

Wallets make up networks â€” players of games, members of DAOs, and users of protocols.

GroupOS's Smart Wallets are ERC-4337 compliant smart wallets, offering full flexibility, programmability and extensibility.

We provide out-of-the-box toolkit groups need to gaslessly onboard and activate wallets to games, applications, and/or protocols.

## Core

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="User Authentication">
    Out-of-the-box package for user authentication with social login and embedded wallet support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Gas Policies">
    Offer gasless user experiences by directly sponsoring users and retroactively bill them as a part of
    the service, directly sponsoring users with ERC20 gas credits, or partnering with
    L2s to sponsor users.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Batch Transactions">
    Batch multiple transactions into one to
    streamline user experience and save gas fees.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Delegated Signers">
    App developers can allow users to add Passkey or additional EoAs as signers
    to increase security and convenience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Directory">
    Track wallets, rewards they earn, and assets they hold.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Analytics">
    Measure your user growth and monitor the gas spend to inform your growth
    strategy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Extensions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Points">
    Reward members with onchain points and optionally unlock new market
    dynamics. with tradable Points (ERC-20).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Badges">
    Segment and reward members based on usage and participation with Badges.
    (ERC-1155).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Direct transfer
You can directly transfer tokens into the Accounts from your wallets.

1. In your Dashboard, go to **Accounts** tab.
2. Find the Account and copy the Account address.
![Directory](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/4ea34fb1-0724-4b19-a3e3-3f75441b50ea.png)
3. Use the Account address as a recipient address in the wallet app of your choice.

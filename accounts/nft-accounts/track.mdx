## Account Directory

You can track Accounts' activities, token ownership, and gas usage via Account Directory.

1. In the Group Dashboard, go to the Accounts tab.
2. Select the Account you wish to view more information about.

**Coming soon**

- Analytics to track growth
- Mint and burn Accounts from the Directory

## Developer Logs

Monitor the distribution and updating of the NFT Accounts via Logs.

1. In the Dashboard, go to **Logs**.
2. Filter by the Account on the side bar.

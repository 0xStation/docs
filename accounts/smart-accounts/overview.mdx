Accounts make up networks â€” players of games, members of DAOs, and users of protocols.

GroupOS's Smart Accounts are ERC-4337 compliant smart accounts, offering full flexibility, programmability and extensibility.

We provide out-of-the-box toolkit groups need to gaslessly onboard and activate accounts to games, applications, and/or protocols.

## Core

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Gas Credits">
    Our toolkit provides app developers with several ways to offer gasless user
    experiences: directly sponsor users and retroactively bill them as a part of
    the service, directly sponsor users with ERC20 gas credits, and partner with
    L2s to sponsor users.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Batch Transactions">
    With smart accounts, dApps can batch multiple transactions into one to
    streamline user experience and save gas fees.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Delegated Signers">
    App developers can allow users to add Passkey or additional EoAs as signers
    to increase security and convenience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Directory">
    Track accounts, rewards they earn, and assets they hold.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Analytics">
    Measure your user growth and monitor the gas spend to inform your growth
    strategy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Extensions

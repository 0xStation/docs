title: "Gas Pass"
---

Gas Passes are branded [NFT Accounts](/nft-accounts/overview). Fill Cards with gas credits to sponsor transactions for your most engaged users.

### Create and configure Gas Pass

1. Go to the ‘Accounts’ tab in the Dashboard.
2. Click ‘+ New Account Group’.
3. Fill out the Account Group details. Click ‘Next’.
   <Info>If you look to deploy a test collection, select Goerli.</Info>

![Accounts.png](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/fe791874-a65e-4b97-be8b-9203bb4c0596.png)

4. In the ‘Connected NFT’ section, customize the visuals and details of the NFTs. Click ‘Done’ to create the NFT collection and the Account Group.

![Accounts.png](https://station-images.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/bad9cd44-dfce-4c48-9f77-a3bdb623ff89.png)

The dashboard is dedicated to analyzing your gas subsidization program. 

- **Top Spenders:** Shows the accounts that used the most gas in a set time period, with a list and visualizations. Find patterns, trends, and important contributors to gas consumption.
- **Top Policies:** Illustrates how different policies are being leveraged, their effectiveness, and their alignment with network objectives.

The information provided is a snapshot of current usage and an actionable insight that guides the continuous improvement and effectiveness of the gas subsidization program.
